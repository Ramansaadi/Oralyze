What is Oralyze?

Oralyze is a breakthrough digital health platform that uses advanced AI technology to analyze your oral health through simple smartphone scans. Our proprietary algorithms can detect early signs of dental issues, track your oral hygiene progress, and provide personalized recommendations for better dental health.

By combining cutting-edge computer vision with medical-grade accuracy, Oralyze brings professional dental insights directly to your fingertips, making preventive oral care accessible to everyone.

How It Works

1. Scan

Simply open the app and scan your mouth using your smartphone camera. Our AI guides you through the perfect angle for accurate results.

2. Analyze

Our advanced AI algorithms process your scan in seconds, detecting potential issues and analyzing your overall oral health status.

3. Get Insights

Receive detailed reports, personalized recommendations, and track your progress over time to maintain optimal oral health.
